About Us

Anhad Shakti Foundation, an active private not-for-profit company, was established on 04 January 2024 in Dulot Ahir, Haryana.

Its leadership includes Shailja Yadav (Director), Sushila (Director), Parikshit (Director).


It is a community-driven organization dedicated to creating a positive impact through social service, cultural preservation, and environmental initiatives.
The foundation actively works in areas such as tree plantation, women empowerment, and community development, while also promoting spiritual growth and moral values(Sanskar) through various programs and events.
Through initiatives like Shrimad Bhagavad Gita Mahayagya, awareness drives, and cultural activities,the organization aims to strengthen the roots of tradition while addressing modern societal needs.
With a strong belief in seva( Service), Sanskar ( Values), and sustainability, Anhad Shakti Foundation strives to build a more aware, empowered, and harmonious society.
